\begin{definition}
A Topological Space blah $(\mathcal{X}, \mathcal{T})$ is a set $\mathcal{X}$ with a family $\mathcal{T}$ of subsets of $\mathcal{T}$ such that:
\begin{enumerate}
 \item $\mathcal{X} \in \mathcal{T}$
\end{enumerate}
\end{definition}
